Astroteens

A student-led 501(c)(3) non profit organization, Astroteens is started to create a community of like minded people who share a common interest in astronomy and its related discipline to learn and explore the universe beyond the Earthâ€™s atmosphere. We at Astroteens give the future a voice. We give students passionate about astronomy and its related discipline an opportunity to express their creativity, ideas and issues that are important to them. In our community all astrophiles can participate in some way or another, be it research or guidance from our mentors.
